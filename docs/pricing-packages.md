### What's included
- Monthly bookkeeping (income & expenses categorised)
- Bank reconciliation

- VAT201 return preparation & submission

### How it works
1. Client signs up at finpal.online
2. We set up their cloud accounting profile
3. Each month: client shares bank statements, invoices, receipts (WhatsApp or email)
4. Our AI processes and categorises all transactions
5. Human review and sign-off
6. Client receives reports by the 7th of each month
